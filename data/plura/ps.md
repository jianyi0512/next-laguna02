#### 概要
* 45W/60W power output
* Auto selecting 110 – 250 V AC
* Status monitor and SNMP features via IE – module
* Overload protection
* Failure relay
* Hot swappable
* 2-pole ON/OFF switch
* 2-pole-fuse-holder

#### 仕様
* サイズ &emsp;104.1(W) x 165.1(D) x 43.2(H) mm  
* 重量&emsp;0.5Kg  
* Power 220/110
